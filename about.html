About Me

Personal Information

Welcome! My name is Ngcebo Majozi I am a 22 year old analytical chemistry graduate from Durban, Kwa-Zulu Natal. I was born and raised in Durban, at the age of 10, I moved to Ladysmith to live with my granny, where I went on to finish my Highschool studies. I'm an aspiring web developer looking to better my skills and knowledge in the field of web development. I focus primarily on front-end development with the hope of being a fullstack developer. I am well versed in both HTML and CSS with a fairly good knowledge of javascript.

Education

I completed my Highschool studies in a small town in the inner Kwa-Zulu Natal region called Dundee known as Dundee Highschool. My studies here composed of; Physical Sciences, Mathematics, Life Sciences and Geography to name a few. My tertiary education was completed at the Durban University of technology, where I obtained my Diploma in Analytical Chemistry in 2020. My courses were mainly chemistry orientated with courses such as; inorganic chemistry, organic chemistry, physical chemistry as well as, electroanalytical chemistry. I am also currently doing an online course with Freecodecamp.org that helps me with my web development training.

Experience

I currently have 1 year of experience within a commercial laboratory setting, where I completed multiple routine and non-routine analysis to analysis a plethora of samples from water to soil, fertilizers, sugar and even animal feed. I am well-versed with a multitude of instrumentation and a variety of analytical methods. I have multiple test and practice websites that showcase my knowledge of both HTML and CSS.
